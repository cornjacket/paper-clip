Activity & Events records mutating actions, heartbeat state changes, cost events, approvals, comments, and work products as durable activity — so operators can audit what happened and why.

## Connections
Provides the immutable audit log behind [[governance-and-approvals]] and the [[tasks-and-tickets|ticket system]]; every action ties back to an actor via [[identity-and-access]]. Cost events feed [[budget-and-cost-control]].
