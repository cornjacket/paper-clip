Routines handle recurring work (customer support, social, reports) on a schedule — no manual kick-offs.

## Details
- Triggers: **cron**, **webhook**, and **API**.
- Concurrency and catch-up policies.
- Each routine execution creates a tracked [[tasks-and-tickets|issue]] and wakes the assigned agent via [[heartbeats]].

## Connections
Supervised under [[governance-and-approvals]] and metered by [[budget-and-cost-control]].
